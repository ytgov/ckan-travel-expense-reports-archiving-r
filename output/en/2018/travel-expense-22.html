Travel expense report for Federal, Provincial and Territorial Health Ministers' meeting, October 19 to 20, 2017

Department: Health and Social Services
Location: Edmonton, Alberta
Purpose of this trip: Minister Frost, in her role as Minister of Health and Social Services, joined her federal, provincial and territorial counterparts for productive discussions on a variety of health-related priorities, including efforts to address opioids and the overdose crisis, improving indigenous health outcomes, and issues of health and safety concerning the legalization and regulation of cannabis. Minister Frost represented Yukon priorities in these discussions.

Participants:

  • Pauline Frost, Minister of Health and Social Services
