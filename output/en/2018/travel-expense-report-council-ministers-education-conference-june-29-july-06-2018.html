Travel expense report for the Council of Ministers of Education Conference, June 29 to July 06, 2018.

Department: Education
Location: Vancouver, British Colombia 
Purpose of the trip: Minister of Education Tracy-Anne McPhee participated in the annual meeting of Council of Ministers of Education, Canada (CMEC) on July 5-6, 2018 in Vancouver, BC. Discussions focused on Indigenous education from the early years to postsecondary studies, as well as global competencies, well-being in schools and United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG 4) in Canada, which relates to the importance of an inclusive, quality education and lifelong learning.
