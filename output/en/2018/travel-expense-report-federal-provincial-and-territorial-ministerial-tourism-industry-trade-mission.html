Travel expense report for the Federal, Provincial and Territorial Ministerial Tourism Industry Trade Mission, June 21 to 30, 2018

Deparment: Tourism and Culture 
Location: China
Purpose of the trip: The Tourism trade mission to China brought a delegation of provincial and territorial ministers and more than two dozen Canadian tourism businesses, including 3 Yukon tourism operators, to Beijing, Hangzhou and Shanghai for business development activities. Throughout the mission, the Minister met with a diverse group of Chinese tourism operators, travel agents, business leaders and government officials to build relationships with key stakeholders in the Chinese tourism market and showcase Yukon’s unique tourism offerings. 

Due to unforeseen circumstances and time constraints, Minister Mostyn attended the China trip on behalf of Minister Dendys. Because of time constraints, Minister Mostyn had to travel to Vancouver on June 11-12 to obtain a Chinese visa. These costs are reflected in this report.
