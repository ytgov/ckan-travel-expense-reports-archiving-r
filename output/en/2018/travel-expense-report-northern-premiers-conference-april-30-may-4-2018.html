Travel expense report for the Northern Premiers' Conference, April 30 to May 4, 2018

Department: Executive Council Office
Location: Ottawa, Ontario
Purpose of the trip: The three northern premiers met in Ottawa to discuss a joint vision for Canada’s North. Premiers also met with Prime Minister Trudeau, Ministers Bennett, McKenna, Morneau and Sohi to advance territorial interests with the federal government on infrastructure funding flexibility, carbon pricing and the Arctic Policy Framework.

Participants

  • Sandy Silver, Premier
  • David Morrison, Chief of Staff, Cabinet Office
  • Emily Farrell, Executive Assistant
