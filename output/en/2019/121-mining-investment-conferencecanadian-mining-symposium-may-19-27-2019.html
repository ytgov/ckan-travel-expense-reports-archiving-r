121 Mining Investment Conference/Canadian Mining Symposium, May 19 to 27, 2019

Department: Finance

Location:  London, United Kingdom/Vancouver, British Columbia 

Purpose of the trip: In partnership with the Yukon Mining Alliance and Yukon mining companies, Premier Silver promoted mining investment opportunities in Yukon at the 121 Mining Investment Conference and at the Canadian Mining Symposium hosted by The Northern Miner. Premier Silver also met with the Canadian High Commissioner to the United Kingdom on the investment potential for Yukon businesses in the London market.

  • David Morrison, Chief of Staff
