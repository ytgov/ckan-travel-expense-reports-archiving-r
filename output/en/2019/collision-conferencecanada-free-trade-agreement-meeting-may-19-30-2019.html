Collision Conference/Canada Free Trade Agreement meeting, May 19 to 30, 2019

Department: Economic Development

Location: Toronto, Ontario/Halifax, Nova Scotia 

Purpose of the trip: Minister Pillai travelled to Toronto with a Yukon delegation to attend Collision Conference, North America’s fastest-growing tech conference to promote Yukon and local startups.

He then continued on to Halifax to attend the Federal, Provincial and Territorial Ministers Committee on Internal Trade: Canada Free Trade Agreement Meeting for discussions on various trade-related matters.
