Travel expense report for the Council of Ministers of Education, July 22 to 26, 2019

Department: Education

Location: Victoria, British Columbia

Purpose of the trip: Minister McPhee joined provincial and territorial Ministers of Education for the annual meeting of the Council of Ministers of Education Canada, held in Victoria, BC. The main focus was indigenous education and reconciliation, and reimagining the future of K-12 and postsecondary education in the context of the changing nature of work.
