Federal Ministers meeting/Investment attraction meeting, June 16 to 21, 2019

Department: Economic Development

Location: Ottawa, Ontario/Toronto, Ontario

Purpose of the trip: Minister Pillai travelled to Ottawa to meet with several federal Ministers to advance Yukon’s priorities on topics including infrastructure, Faro mine site, and business development.

Minister Pillai then continued on to Toronto to meet with several senior level executives to discuss investment opportunities in Yukon.
