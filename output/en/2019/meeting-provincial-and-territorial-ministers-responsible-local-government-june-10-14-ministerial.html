Meeting for Provincial and Territorial Ministers responsible for Local Government, June 10 to 14 / Ministerial Conference on the Canadian Francophonie, June 26 to 29, 2019

Department: Cabinet

Location: Halifax, Nova Scotia/ Iqaluit, Nunavut

Purpose of the trip: Provincial and Territorial Ministers responsible for local government reaffirmed their commitment to work together to strengthen the resiliency and sustainability of communities across Canada. Ministers discussed common priorities, such as climate change adaptation and municipal infrastructure and asset management, and their continued work to address these priorities in the coming year.
