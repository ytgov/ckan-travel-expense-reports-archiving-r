Federal, Provincial and Territorial Ministers Responsible for Seniors Forum, May 20 to 23, 2019

Department: Health and Social Services

Location: Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island

Purpose of the trip: The Federal, Provincial and Territorial (FPT) Ministers Responsible for Seniors Forum met in Charlottetown, PEI to discuss key priorities and meaningful ways to support current and future generations of Canadian seniors. Minister Frost also chaired a Northern Ministers’ Meeting to discuss Northern priorities related to seniors and other health care issues.
