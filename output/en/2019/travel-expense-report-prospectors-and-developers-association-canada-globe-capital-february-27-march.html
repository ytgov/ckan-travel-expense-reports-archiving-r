Travel expense report for Prospectors and Developers Association of Canada/ Globe Capital, February 27 to March 6, 2019

Department: Energy Mines and Resources
Location: Toronto, Ontario
Purpose of the trip: PDAC is an annual international convention held in Toronto. It provides an opportunity for Government of Yukon to share information about Yukon’s mineral potential with investors and industry. Minister Pillai attended meetings and forums with key mineral companies and investors, as well as federal, territorial and international counterparts.

Globe Capital 2019: Minister Pillai participated in a panel discussion on energy, titled: “Opportunity Spotlight: The North Vortex – Unfreezing Sustainable Investment Opportunities in Canada’s North”

  • Kim Stavert, Executive Assistant
