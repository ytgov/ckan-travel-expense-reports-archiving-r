Travel expense report for Yukon Days/ Canadian Council of Fisheries and Aquaculture Ministers, December 2 to 6, 2018

Department: Environment/ Health and Human Services
Location: Ottawa, ON/St-John, NL
Purpose of the trip: Minister Frost joined Premier Silver and Minister McPhee, the Grand Chief of the Council of Yukon First Nations and several Yukon First Nations Chiefs for meetings with federal Ministers to jointly advocate on key issues important to Yukoners.

Minister Frost travelled on to St. John’s for discussions with her federal, provincial and territorial counterparts on shared priorities for Canada’s fisheries and aquaculture programs.

  • Pauline Frost-Hanberg, Minister
