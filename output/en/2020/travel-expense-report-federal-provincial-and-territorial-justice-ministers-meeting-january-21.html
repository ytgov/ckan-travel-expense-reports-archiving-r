Travel expense report for the Federal, Provincial and Territorial Justice Ministers meeting, January 21 to February 5, 2020

Department: Justice

Location: Victoria and Vancouver, British Columbia

Purpose of the trip: Minister McPhee travelled to Victoria to participate in a meeting of Canada’s Federal, Provincial and Territorial Justice Ministers about key issues. Topics included an acknowledgment of the MMIWG final report Calls for Justice and restorative justice. While in BC she also participated in Round-up.
