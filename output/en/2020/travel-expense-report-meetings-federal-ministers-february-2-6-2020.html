Travel expense report for the meetings with Federal Ministers, February 2 to 6, 2020

Department: Community Services

Location: Ottawa, Ontario

Purpose of the trip: Minister Streicker and his Executive Assistant Monica Nordling attended several meetings with Federal Ministers in Ottawa including meetings with Minister McKenna, Minister Guilbeault, Minister Blair and Minister Joly. Topics of discussion included the federal Investing in Canada Infrastructure Program and Disaster Mitigation and Adaptation Fund, the 2020 Arctic Winter Games, and the Canada-Yukon Agreement under the Official Languages Act.
