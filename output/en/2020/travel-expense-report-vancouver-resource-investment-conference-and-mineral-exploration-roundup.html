Travel expense report for the Vancouver Resource Investment Conference and the Mineral Exploration Roundup, January 18 to 23, 2020

Department: Energy, Mines and Resources 

Location: Vancouver, British Columbia

Purpose of the trip: Premier Silver and Minister Pillai joined the Yukon delegation at the Vancouver Resource Investment Conference and the  Mineral Exploration Roundup. The purpose was to share Yukon’s mineral potential, geology and regulatory regime and meet with companies and organizations with an interest in Yukon’s mineral industry.

  • Sandy Silver, Premier
  • Ranj Pillai, Minister 
  • Kim Stavert, Executive Assistant
  • Emily Farrell, Executive Assistant
