Travel expense report for Yukon Days, November 30 to December 6, 2019

Department: Health and Social Services, and Education

Location: Ottawa, Ontario

Purpose of the trip: Ministers McPhee and Frost travelled to Ottawa for trilateral meetings to present priorities shared by the Yukon government and Yukon First Nations governments to federal Ministers. The themes for this year’s discussions included First Nations language revitalization, child welfare, education and community safety.

Participants

  • Tracy-Anne McPhee, Minister 
  • Pauline Frost, Minister 
  • Emily Farrell, Executive Assistant
