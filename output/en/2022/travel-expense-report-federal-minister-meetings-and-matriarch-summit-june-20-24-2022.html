Travel expense report for the Federal minister meetings and Matriarch Summit, June 20 to 24, 2022

Department: Education, Women and Gender Equity Directorate

Location: Ottawa, Winnipeg

Purpose of the trip: Minister McLean had meetings with federal ministers and MP Hanley. Discussions centred around the evolution of education in the territory with Re-imagining Special and Inclusive Education and the First Nations School Board.

Minister McLean then travelled to the Matriarch Summit. The event featured presentations and meetings with industry leadership on gender equity rights and Indigenous leadership throughout the country.
