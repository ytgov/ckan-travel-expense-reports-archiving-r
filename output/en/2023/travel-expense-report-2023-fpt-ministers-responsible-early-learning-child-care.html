Travel expense report for the 2023 FPT Ministers Responsible for Early Learning and Child Care, July 12 to July 14, 2023

Location

Purpose of the trip

Minister McLean participated in meetings with federal, provincial and territorial counterparts. Discussions focussed on the strategy framework for recruitment, retention and recognition of early learning and childcare professionals. Her Ministerial Advisor, Paolo Gallina, accompanied her in these meetings.
