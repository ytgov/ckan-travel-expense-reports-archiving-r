Travel expense report for the 2023 FPT Ministers Responsible Status of Women, July 29 to August 02

Location

Charlottetown, PEI

Department

Women and Gender Equity Directorate

Purpose of the trip

Minister McLean participated in meetings with federal, provincial and territorial counterparts. Discussions focussed on women's equal participation in the economy and work being done across the country to end gender-based violence. Her Ministerial Advisor, Paolo Gallina, accompanied her in these meetings.
