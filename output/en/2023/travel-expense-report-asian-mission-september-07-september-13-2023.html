Travel expense report for the Asian mission, September 07 to September 13, 2023

Location

Tokyo, Asia

Department

Tourism and Culture and Energy, Mines and Resources

Participant

John Streicker, Minister forTourism and Culture and Energy, Mines and Resources

Purpose of the trip

Minister Streicker joined the Asia mission with the Premier and a Yukon business delegation for meetings in Tokyo, Japan. Minister Streicker engaged with several travel agencies including Destination Canada, H.I.S. World Global and ST World. As well, Minister Streicker accompanied the Premier for meetings with the Canadian Ambassador to Japan, the Japanese Ambassador in charge of Arctic Affairs, International Affairs and International Economic Affairs,  Japan Overseas Infrastructure Investment (JOIN) and mining industry companies, Sumitomo Corporation, Nittetsu Mining and Mitsubishi Materials.

Total travel expenditures for participant

Transportation: $3,753.09

Other, such as per diems and taxi fares: $1,053.16

Total costs for this trip: $6,616.80
