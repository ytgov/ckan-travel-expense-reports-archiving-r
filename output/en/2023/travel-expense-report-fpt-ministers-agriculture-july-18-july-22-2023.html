Travel expense report for the FPT Ministers of Agriculture, July 18 to July 22, 2023

Department: Energy, Mines and Resources

Location: Frederiction, New Brunswick, Canada

Purpose of the trip: Minister Streicker and Moira Lassen, Ministerial Advisor attended the FPT Ministers of Agriculture in Fredericton, New Brunswick. Minister Streicker met with federal, provincial, and territorial government officials as well as the Canadian Federation of Agriculture. Minister Streicker also provided remarks on the transition of the Yukon to co-host the 2024 FPT Ministers Agriculture Meeting in the summer of 2024.
