Travel expense report for FPT Ministers Responsible for Culture and Heritage, June 26 to 30, 2023

Department: Tourism & Culture

Location: Toronto

Purpose of the trip: Minister Streicker and Moira Lassen, Ministerial Advisor attended the FPT Ministers and Deputy Ministers’ Responsible for Culture and Heritage meeting in Toronto, Ontario. Minister Streicker met with federal, provincial and territorial government officials as well as National Indigenous Organizations leadership. Minister Streicker also provided remarks on the transition of the Yukon to co-host the 2024 FPT Ministers Meeting for Culture and Heritage mid-2024.

  • Moira Lassen, Ministerial Advisor
