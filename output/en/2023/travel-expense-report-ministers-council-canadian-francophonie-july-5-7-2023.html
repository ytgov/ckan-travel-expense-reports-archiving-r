Travel expense report for the Ministers Council on the Canadian Francophonie, July 5 to 7, 2023

Location: Vancouver

Purpose of the trip: Minister Streicker and Moira Lassen, Ministerial Advisor attended the Ministers Council on the Canadian Francophonie in Vancouver, British Columbia.
