Travel expense report for the PT Ministers Responsible for Local Government, June 1 to 7, 2023

Department: Community Services 

Location: Ottawa

Purpose of the trip: The purpose of the travel was to meet with the Minister Mostyn’s elected counterparts from across the country.  The Ministers shared their priorities, including local governance, planning for climate change adaptation and associated local infrastructure, and increasing housing supply as well as local services to support growth.
