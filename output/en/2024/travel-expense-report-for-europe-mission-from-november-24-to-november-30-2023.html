Travel expense report for Europe mission from November 24 to November 30, 2023

Department: ECO, EcDev and EMR

Location: Frankfurt and London

Purpose of the trip: 

Premier Pillai and Principal Secretary Kim Stavert, completed a mission to Europe where they attended mining conferences and held meetings with government and business leaders to promote opportunities in the Yukon, notably as a secure and stable supply for the critical minerals that the world needs to support the clean energy transition. This mission was a valuable step for the Government of Yukon to promote innovation and collaboration in critical minerals and create lasting relationships to increase investments in the territory. It also provided opportunities to emphasize First Nations involvement in the Yukon’s mining industry and to share updates on the progress of the co-development of new minerals legislation, which supports a modern and sustainable mining industry.
