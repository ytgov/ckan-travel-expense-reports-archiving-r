Travel expense report for the Round Up Conference, January 20 to January 25, 2024

Department: Energy, Mines & Resources

Location: Vancouver 

Purpose of the trip: Minister Streicker and Ministerial Advisor, Moira Lassen travelled to Vancouver, BC for both the Vancouver Resources Investment Conference (VRIC) and AME RoundUp from January 20 to 25, 2024.  Minister Streicker met with government officials from various jurisdictions and First Nations as well multiple mining companies. Minister Streicker also provided a welcoming remark at Invest Yukon Reception, opening remarks at the Tombstone GoldRush Breakfast, and gave a welcome address at the Yukon Night event.
