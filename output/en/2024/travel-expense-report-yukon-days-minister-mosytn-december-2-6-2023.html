Travel expense report for Yukon Days for Minister Mostyn, December 2 to 6, 2023

Location

 Ottawa, Ontario

Department

Community Services

Purpose of the trip

Minister Mostyn travelled to Ottawa as part of the Yukon Government’s ‘Yukon Days’ contingent. He attended the Yukon Forum that was held there, met with Housing Minister Sean Fraser regarding infrastructure and housing, and also with Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister of Emergency Preparedness MP Sherry Romanado, on the issue of natural disasters and other possible emergencies

Participants

  • Richard Mostyn, Minister

Total travel expenditures for participant

Airfare: $2,414.18
Accommodation: $2,060.82
Other, such as per diems and taxi fares: $478.44

Total costs for this trip: $4,953.44
