Travel expense report for AME Round up and First Ministers Meeting, January 24 to January 30

Department: Energy, Mines and resources / Executive Council Office 

Location: Vancouver, British Columbia / Ottawa, Ontario 

Purpose of the trip: Premier Currie Dixon attended AME Roundup in Vancouver to promote mineral exploration and development in the Yukon, and the Council of the Federation/First Ministers’ Meeting in Ottawa to discuss concrete actions to build a more prosperous territory and country. Chief of Staff, Danny Macdonald and Principal Secretary, Carleen Kerr attended to support Ministers and Premier.
