Travel expense report for the Ministers’ Meeting for Early Learning and Childcare and AME Roundup, January 19 to 24, 2020

Department: Education

Location: Vancouver, British Columbia and Ottawa, Ontario

Purpose of the trip: Minister Frost and her Executive Assistant Pavlina Sudrich attended the Ministers’ Meeting for Early Learning and Childcare in Ottawa, Ontario. Topics of discussion included the importance of investments in early learning, opportunities for collaboration with the federal government on before and after school care and support for early childhood educators. On her way to Ottawa, Minister Frost attended events at AME Roundup 2020 in Vancouver BC.
