Travel expense report for the FPT Ministers of Infrastructure Meeting, June 19 to 23, 2023

Department: Community Services 

Location: Whistler

Purpose of the trip: The purpose of the travel was to attend an FPT meeting with federal Minister Dominic Leblanc, Minister of Intergovernmental Affairs, Infrastructure and Communities. Ministers discussed the considerable number of investments and projects completed through the Investing in Canada Infrastructure Program. They also exchanged ways to collaboratively address the infrastructure challenges Canada will face in the coming years.

  • Brandon Kassbaum, Ministerial Advisor
